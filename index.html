About

I'm an AI/ML engineer who builds intelligent systems end to end — the data pipelines that feed them, the models at their core, and the production software that ships them. Over 4+ years I've gone from machine learning, to deep data engineering (Google Cloud Professional ML Engineer, Professional Data Engineer & Associate Cloud Engineer certified), to building AI-native platforms powered by LLMs and multi-agent systems. Because I own the whole stack, I can take an idea from raw data to a deployed product without handing it off three times.

Currently at BlueArc (built by risk and trust leaders from Google, eBay and WePay), I architected a risk-decisioning layer that evaluates 2M+ business signals monthly, cutting policy deployment from months to hours. I also build the LLM agents and ML models behind our fraud, KYB, ad and marketplace risk products, protecting significant ad spend and transaction volume for major names in advertising, e-commerce and payments.

What I focus on: Generative AI & agents — LLM agents and multi-agent systems (LangChain, LangGraph, Google ADK), plus RAG, knowledge graphs, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, evaluation, inference optimization (quantization, distillation) and LLM tracing (Langfuse). Machine learning — scoring and decisioning models spanning anomaly detection and classification, gradient-boosting and graph-based methods. Data & MLOps — Dataflow, Apache Beam, Airflow and BigQuery, plus CI/CD, monitoring and observability (Prometheus, Grafana, Cloud Monitoring).

The through-line isn't a single domain — it's taking ownership of hard problems end to end, working closely with the customers who rely on what I build. I've applied that across risk, healthcare and finance. I hold an MS in Artificial Intelligence and an Integrated MTech in Computer Science, and have published research including 4EV (text-to-video generation) and VetMamba (efficient long-sequence modeling with the Mamba architecture). If you're building something ambitious with AI, I'd love to connect.
